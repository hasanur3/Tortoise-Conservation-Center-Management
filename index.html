About the Project

This system helps manage tortoise records, monitor enclosures, track breeding programs, and ensure health and environmental control within the conservation center.

Key Features

Tortoise Records

Store details for each tortoise: ID, age, species, health, and care history.

Enclosure Monitoring

Live data on temperature, humidity, and water quality using IoT devices.

Breeding & Health

Manage breeding logs, hatching records, vet visits, and medical treatments.
